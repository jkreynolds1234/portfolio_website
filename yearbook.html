Yearbook App

I solely coded the front and backends of a yearbook app for UC Davis Students to form a secure account, upload a photo, develop a bio, and search for profiles. I prototyped and iterated mockups using Figma. I used JavaScript, SQLite3, HTML5, CSS, React.js, Node.js, HTTP, AJAX, jQuery, and Google API.
