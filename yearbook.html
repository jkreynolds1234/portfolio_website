Yearbook App

I independently designed and coded the front and back ends of a Yearbook app for UC Davis students (30,000+ students) to create an account, upload a photo, create bio, and search for profiles. I used Javascript, SQLite3, HTML5, CSS, React.js, Node.js, HTTP, AJAX, jQuery, and Google API. I used inDesign for the design mock-ups of the web app.
